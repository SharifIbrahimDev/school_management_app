## Introduction
Welcome to the School Management App, a comprehensive solution designed to streamline administrative tasks, enhance communication, and improve academic tracking for schools. This guide provides detailed instructions for all user roles: Proprietors, Principals, Bursars, Teachers, and Parents.

## Getting Started

### Login and Authentication
1.  **Launch the App**: Open the application on your device.
2.  **Enter Credentials**: Input your registered email address and password.
3.  **Select Role (if applicable)**: If you have multiple roles (e.g., Teacher and Parent), you may be prompted to select which dashboard to view.
4.  **Forgot Password**: If you cannot remember your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen to reset it via your email.

## For Proprietors
*The Proprietor view focuses on high-level oversight, school configuration, and financial health.*

### Proprietor Dashboard
Your centralized hub for school metrics.
*   **Key Metrics**: View total student enrollment, total staff count, and summary financial data (total revenue/outstanding fees) at a glance.
*   **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to add new sessions, view financial reports, or manage school settings.

### School & Session Management
*   **Academic Sessions**: Create and manage academic years (e.g., 2023/2024). Set the current active session.
*   **Terms**: Define terms (First, Second, Third) within a session.
*   **Sections**: configure sections (e.g., Primary, Secondary) and assign heads of sections.

### Financial Oversight
*   **Reports**: Access detailed financial reports to track income trends.
*   **Fee Structures**: Approve or review fee structures created by the Bursar.

---

## For Principals
*The Principal view focuses on daily school operations, academic integrity, and personnel management.*

### Principal Dashboard
Snapshot of daily attendance and academic progress.
*   **Attendance Summary**: See daily student and staff attendance rates.
*   **Upcoming Events**: View the school calendar and efficient notices.

### Staff & Student Management
*   **User Directory**: Search for any student or staff member. View detailed profiles and academic history.
*   **Class Allocation**: Assign class teachers to specific classes.
*   **Discipline**: View or log disciplinary records (if enabled).

### Academic Planning
*   **Subjects**: Manage the curriculum by adding subjects to specific classes.
*   **Timetables**: View class timetables to ensure proper scheduling.
*   **Reports Approval**: Review and approve student result sheets before they are published to parents.

---

## For Bursars
*The Bursar view is dedicated to financial transactions, invoicing, and record-keeping.*

### Bursar Dashboard
Focused on cash flow and outstanding payments.
*   **Fee Statistics**: Visual charts showing collected vs. outcome fees.
*   **Recent Transactions**: A list of the latest payments recorded.

### Fee Management
*   **Create Fees**: Set up fee items (Tuition, Uniform, Books) for specific classes or sections.
*   **Invoices**: Generate invoices for students for a specific term.

### Recording Payments
1.  Navigate to **Payments** or search for a **Student**.
2.  Select the specific fee item being paid for.
3.  Enter the amount, payment method (Cash, Transfer, POS), and date.
4.  **Receipts**: The system automatically generates a digital receipt which can be shared or printed.

### Transaction History
*   View a comprehensive log of all financial transactions.
*   Filter by date, payment method, or student to reconcile accounts.

## For Teachers
*The Teacher view streamlines classroom management, grading, and student interaction.*

### Teacher Dashboard
Your daily organizer.
*   **My Class**: Quick access to your assigned class list.
*   **Today's Timetable**: See your teaching schedule for the day.

### Class Management
*   **Student List**: View all students in your class with their contact details.
*   **Subject Management**: Access subjects you are assigned to teach.

### Taking Attendance
1.  Go to the **Attendance** section.
2.  Select the date and class.
3.  Mark students as Present, Absent, or Late.
4.  Submit to save the record.

### Grading & Reports
*   **Input Scores**: Enter scores for Tests, Assignments, and Exams for each subject.
*   **Compute Results**: The system automatically calculates totals and grades.
*   **Remarks**: Add individual comments on student performance for report cards.
